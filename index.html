About Us

Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Step into Gulmolas Coffee and Food in Bemina, Srinagar, where each flavor unveils a unique story. Amidst Kashmir's picturesque landscapes, our restaurant offers an unforgettable culinary experience. Our menu pays homage to Kashmiri culture, weaving centuries-old traditions into every dish. From the aromatic Kahwa to traditional Wazwan delicacies, each sip and bite transports you to culinary bliss. With a dedication to excellence, we preserve Kashmiri authenticity while adding contemporary flair. Join us for a gastronomic journey where flavors narrate tales of heritage and indulgence. At Gulmolas, every visit promises an enchanting exploration of Kashmir's culinary delights.
